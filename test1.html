Hanako-Kun

Hanako (花子 Hanako?), whose real name is Yugi Amane (柚木普 Yugi Amane?) A ghost haunting the old building of Kamome Academy and is the titular protagonist of the series
He resides on the third floor female bathroom, in the toilet of the third stall.
Hanako-san will appear to grant your wish.
Hanako governs over the Seven Mysteries as School Mystery Number 7.
